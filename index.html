About Northwind Supply Co.

Northwind Supply Co. distributes industrial equipment, tooling, and safety supplies to manufacturing and construction businesses across the western United States. The company's catalog spans power tools, fasteners, protective equipment, and fleet maintenance parts, delivered through regional warehouses and a same-week shipping guarantee. Northwind also operates a managed-inventory program for enterprise accounts, and a consulting arm that helps clients standardize procurement across multiple facilities. In recent years the company has invested heavily in a self-serve ordering portal and route-optimized delivery fleet, aiming to reduce average fulfillment time for repeat industrial customers.
